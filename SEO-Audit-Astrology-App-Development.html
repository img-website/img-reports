3. Schema / Structured Data

Detected (full set): @graph [Organization, WebSite, WebPage], Service, Product (+AggregateRating 4.7/138), FAQPage (DB), BreadcrumbList (DB). Good coverage — better than the OTT page.

HIGH 3.1 — Conflicting / unsubstantiated rating 
Schema:        "ratingValue": "4.7", "ratingCount": "138"
Page shows:    "4.7/5"  (one section)  AND  "4.5/5 Average Rating"  (another)

The page displays two different ratings (4.7/5 and 4.5/5), the schema count (138) appears nowhere, and the value differs from the sitewide 4.5/5. Google requires structured-data ratings to match a single, genuine, visible rating. This is better than the Home Service/Dating pages (where nothing matched) but still inconsistent.

Solution: pick one genuine rating + count, show it visibly, and make schema match it exactly. Remove the conflicting second value. Standardise the rating across all pages.
HIGH 3.2 — Product schema for a service
A Service node already exists. Drop Product; attach the (corrected, truthful) aggregateRating to the Service or Organization node instead.
MEDIUM 3.3 — Organization sameAs empty in @graph
Populate Organization sameAs (Service node already lists socials).
