2. Page-family context (architecture)

Ye URL on-demand cluster ka hub hai (“hub & spoke”). Hub baaki spoke pages ko descriptive anchors ke saath link karta hai — food delivery, grocery, taxi, doctor-on-demand, home service, laundry, medicine, car wash, alcohol, gift. Ye achhi internal-link architecture hai aur topical authority ke liye accha hai. GOOD

  • Intent split healthy hai: hub broad “on-demand app development company” target karta hai; spokes specific verticals → cannibalization risk kam.
  • Spokes pe verify karo (2 jagah URL ≠ folder name): /grocery-app-development actually grocery-delivery-app-development view render karta hai (pageName grocery-delivery-app-development), aur /taxi-app-development-company, taxi-booking-app-development view render karta hai. Confirm karo ki har spoke ka canonical uske apne live URL pe point kare (jaise grocery page ka canonical /grocery-app-development ho, koi dusra slug nahi). Galat slug pe canonical = wo page de-index ho sakta hai.
  • Reciprocal linking: ensure karo ki har spoke wapas hub ko link kare — cluster dono taraf se strong hota hai.

3. Technical SEO

Ye pehle se sahi hai (live verify kiya) — inhe “fix” mat karna:
  • <title> “On-Demand App Development Company | IMG Global Infotech” (~54 chars) aur meta description (~152 chars) — dono sahi length me.
  • Self-referential canonical → clean URL. Robots = INDEX, FOLLOW (production).
  • Purana /on-demand-app-development-company.php → clean URL pe 301 redirect (header check kiya). Internal links asset() use karte hain → clean URLs. Matlab ye duplicate-content bug nahi hai.
  • Single <h1>. Clean URL sitemap.xml me hai (1,041 URLs). og:type + twitter:card sitewide globalMeta se aate hain.
  • Hero image loading="eager" + fetchpriority="high" + width/height set → accha LCP handling. Images WebP hain.

Evidence site: search me …/on-demand-app-development-company.php (aur home-service.php, food-delivery.php, taxi.php, doctor.php, fuel.php…) aata hai, clean URLs nahi.

Evidence Head me "@type":"Product" ke saath "aggregateRating": { "ratingValue":"4.7","ratingCount":"143" } aur empty image hai.
Kaaran Product-style page se template hua; aap service company ho, product seller nahi.
Kyo matter karta hai (a) Galat type — yahan koi Product bik hi nahi raha. (b) Bina kisi real, visible, on-page review system ke self-serving AggregateRating Google ki review-snippet guidelines ke against hai → “spammy structured data” manual action ka aur/ya stars hatne ka risk. Fix Product + AggregateRating block hatao. Agar stars chahiye to ek genuine on-page review/testimonial system banao aur real Review/AggregateRating se markup karo jo visible reviews reflect kare. Warna Organization + Service (pehle se hai) bina ratings ke rakho.

Detail Page ke @graph me ek Organization hai aur sitewide globalSchema bhi Organization + 2 LocalBusiness (Jaipur + Dubai) deta hai. Confirm karo ek hi Organization ho consistent @id ke saath, taaki merge ho duplicate na ho.

5. Content quality — generic vs. organic

Aapne specially poocha: content generic hai kya? Kai jagah — haan. Page ke bones aur length acche hain, par prose salesy/templated lagta hai aur do real defects hain.

P0 “Why choose us” section me galat “taxi” card

Evidence “Why Select IMG Global Infotech for On-Demand App Development Services” me pehle card ka heading “Navigating Taxi Booking App” hai taxi-only copy ke saath (“…passengers as well as drivers… our taxi app solution…”). Ye string sirf is page pe hai — taxi page se paste hua aur heading kabhi badla hi nahi.
Kyo matter Hub page pe copy-paste error lagta hai + trust aur topical coherence kamzor karta hai.
Fix Real hub-level differentiator se replace karo, jaise “Multi-vertical on-demand expertise” ya “Three-panel architecture (customer · provider · admin)” matching copy ke saath.

P1 Generic / hyperbolic / grammar-toota copy

Hero: “…showcase your thoughts to your audience by the on demand delivery app development company.” — grammar toota + keyword-stuffed.
“At IMG Global Infotech, we don’t make apps; we just transform your ideas… to achieve crazy results that you never expected before.” — vague, salesy, credible nahi.
“An on-demand app development company always empowers businesses with their personalized app solutions that can cater to the needs of your business.” — circular filler.
Kaaran Template prose jo keyword density ke liye likha gaya, human reader ke liye nahi.
Fix Intros ko specific aur human banao: real proof (1,200+ projects, 100+ team, 10+ yrs), concrete capabilities (real-time tracking, dispatch, surge pricing, multi-vendor, wallet, 3-panel apps), named outcomes. Repeated “on demand … app development company” padding hatao — phrasing vary karo.

P1 Bina source ke statistics (E-E-A-T & accuracy risk)

Evidence Stat cards me $320B Market Size by 2033, 250B+ App Downloads, 8.58% Annual CAGR — koi source nahi.
Kyo matter Aapne kaha “kuch galat mat batana.” Bina source ke numbers E-E-A-T liability hain aur galat bhi ho sakte hain.
Fix Har figure ke saath reputable source + year do (Statista / Grand View Research / Business Research Insights), ya hata do. Linked, sourced stats AI-citation chances bhi badhate hain.
