3. Root cause — koi bhi page rank kyun nahi ho raha, page 10 par bhi nahi

Jab koi page top 100 me bhi nahi hota (jabki DR-61 domain pehle se dusre pages rank karwa raha hai), to wajah kabhi "aur content chahiye" ya "aur links chahiye" nahi hoti. Wajah in 3 me se hoti hai — aur yahan teeno ek saath hai:

1Doorway / duplicate templated pages → Google inhe filter kar deta hai/seo-audit · /seo-content

15 country pages ek hi template hai jisme bas location ka naam badla gaya hai. Humne measure kiya: har pair ke beech 36–41% same 6-word sequences, aur New York page ke 46% sentences UK page jaise bilkul same hai — jab "New York/USA" vs "UK/London" normalise kar do. Google ise "doorway pages" kehta hai aur ya to (a) sabko ek canonical me consolidate karke baaki drop kar deta hai, ya (b) Crawled – currently not indexed me chhod deta hai. Dono case me → koi ranking nahi.

2Aapke 8 saal purane main page ki cannibalisation/seo · /seo-audit

Aapke /mobile-app-development page ke paas 8 saal ki age, links aur ranking equity hai. Naye pages overlapping queries target kar rahe hai ("mobile app development company", "…in india", "…in jaipur", "…in delhi") bahut similar content ke saath. Google ek hi intent ke liye multiple pages dekhta hai aur established page ko dikhata rehta hai, naye ko ignore karta hai. Naye pages purane ko replace nahi karte — ulta purane ki wajah se dab jaate hai.

3Page-level authority ≈ 0 aur koi genuine local/geo relevance nahi/seo-backlinks · /seo-local · /seo-hreflang

DR 61 ek domain score hai jo zyadatar aapke homepage aur purane pages par hai. Naye location pages ke apne referring domains lagbhag zero hai (Ahrefs me URL Rating confirm karo). Upar se, "…company in New York" target karne wale page par India + Dubai ka address pada hai, koi local phone nahi, koi local case study nahi, aur koi hreflang nahi — to Google ke paas koi signal hi nahi ki ye page New York ke liye relevant hai. Strong domain bhi us page ko nahi bacha sakta jo duplicate bhi ho AUR jiske paas page-level relevance/links bhi na ho.

4. Problem kya NAHI hai — taaki aap yahan paisa barbaad na karo

Ye sab already theek hai
  • Indexing block — koi nahi. robots.txt in URLs ko allow karta hai, robots meta index,follow hai, canonicals self-referencing aur sahi hai.
  • Crawlability / JS rendering — theek hai. Pages fully server-rendered hai (koi important content JavaScript ke peeche chhupa nahi). 18/18 URLs sitemap.xml me hai.
  • Basic on-page meta — titles & meta descriptions har page par unique hai, har page par ek H1, Open Graph + Twitter cards present, aur image alt text present (lagbhag koi missing nahi).
  • AI/GEO crawler access — robots.txt me GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended etc. already allowed hai, aur llms.txt bhi referenced hai. Achhi base hai.
  • Domain authority — DR 61 strong hai. Domain-level par aur generic link-buying se ye specific pages nahi hilenge.

Isliye aapke pichle "content change" aur "SEO team" wale attempts kaam nahi aaye — wo symptom (wording) theek kar rahe the, structural causes (duplication, cannibalisation, per-page relevance/links) nahi.

5. Detailed findings — issue · kyun hua · solution

CriticalPages indexed-but-filtered / index hi nahi ho rahe/seo-audit · /seo-google · /seo-technical
Issue
Pages top 100 me nahi aate. Jab sab kuch technically open hai, iska matlab Google inhe rank na karne ka decision le raha hai — expected GSC statuses: Duplicate, Google chose a different canonical than user, Crawled – currently not indexed, ya Discovered – currently not indexed.
Kyun hua
Google ka duplicate-content consolidation: jab kai pages near-identical ho, wo ek "representative" ko index karta hai aur baaki drop kar deta hai. Doorway-page systems algorithmically demote bhi ho sakte hai.
Solution
  • Google Search Console → Pages (indexing) report kholo; 18 URLs ko status ke hisab se classify karo. 3–4 par URL Inspection → Test live URL chalao.
  • Manual Actions aur Security tabs check karo ("Thin content / Doorway" manual action rule out karne ke liye).
  • Pehle duplication fix karo (neeche), tab hi Request Indexing use karo — clones dobara submit karoge to wo dobara filter ho jaayenge.
CriticalNear-duplicate, doorway-style location pages/seo · /seo-content · /seo-audit
Issue
15 country pages 36–41% exact 6-word phrases share karte hai; NY vs UK = 46% sentences identical (location-swap ke baad). Pages ke beech sirf city/country ka naam alag hai.
Kyun hua
Pages ek hi template se mass-produce kiye gaye har geo ko "capture" karne ke liye — Google ki spam policy me yahi "doorway pages" ki textbook definition hai.
Solution
  • Count drastically kam karo. 17 thin clones ek dusre ko dilute karte hai. Sirf wo locations rakho jinhe aap genuinely unique bana sako aur jahan aapke paas real proof ho (dekho §7).
  • Jo page rakho, uska ≥60–70% body genuinely unique & locally specific banao: local client case studies, names ke saath local testimonials, country-specific pricing/currency, local app-market nuances (jaise regulations, popular platforms), real local point of contact, locally-relevant FAQs.
  • Jo differentiate na kar sako → ek regional page me consolidate karo ya pause karo (§7). 4 markets ke liye unique deep page, 17 clones se behtar hai.
CriticalMain page ke saath keyword cannibalisation/seo · /seo-audit
Issue
/mobile-app-development, …company-in-india, …in-jaipur, …in-delhi sab "mobile app development company" intent ko similar content ke saath chase kar rahe hai.
Kyun hua
Koi keyword map nahi tha. Naye pages already rank kar rahe page ke upar add kiye gaye same/adjacent terms ke liye, to wo apni hi site se compete karte hai.
Solution
Har URL ko ek primary keyword do aur intent differentiate karo (poora map §6 me). Main page ko global pillar rehne do (uski equity ko disturb mat karo). Har location page ko ek distinct, zyada specific query ka owner banao. Descriptive, distinct anchor text ke saath interlink karo — har page par wahi footer block nahi.
HighSchema globally templated hai — har page par galat & misleading/seo-schema
Issue
Har page — New York, UK, Germany, Canada, Australia samet — par identical LocalBusiness markup hai wahi Jaipur (IN) + Dubai (AE) PostalAddress ke saath. Pages me self-serving AggregateRating / Product / Brand markup bhi hai.
Kyun hua
JSON-LD block shared template ka hissa hai aur kabhi localise nahi kiya gaya; company ke apne service pages par review stars laga diye gaye.
Solution
  • Jahan real office nahi hai wahan LocalBusiness declare mat karo. Market/service pages ke liye Organization + Service + areaServed use karo, aur LocalBusiness (sahi verifiable NAP ke saath) sirf India (Jaipur) aur Dubai ke liye rakho.
  • Apne service pages se self-serving AggregateRating hatao — Google ki review-snippet policy apne hi business ke reviews ko apni hi site par markup karne se mana karti hai; ye ignore ya flag ho sakta hai.
  • FAQPage markup rakho & expand karo (India page par hai) — har kept page par unique local FAQs add karo. Organization par proper sameAs entity links lagao.
High15 country pages par koi hreflang / international targeting nahi/seo-hreflang
Issue
Zero hreflang tags. 15 English pages 15 alag countries target kar rahe hai, bina kisi signal ke ki kaunsa page kis market ke liye hai — to ye ek dusre se compete karte duplicates lagte hai.
Kyun hua
International SEO setup kabhi implement hi nahi kiya; pages localised versions ki jagah English-only clones hai.
Solution
  • Important: hreflang same-language conflicts tabhi solve karta hai jab content actually har market ke liye alag ho. To pehle content differentiate karo, fir hreflang lagao.
  • Jo markets rakho, unme reciprocal hreflang implement karo: en-IN, en-US, en-GB, en-AE … plus x-default. Set ke har page ko har dusre page (aur khud ko) list karna chahiye.
  • Jo markets truly localise na kar sako, wahan fake hreflang mat lagao — un pages ko pause/consolidate karo (§7).
HighKoi real local signals nahi (aur India local under-used)/seo-local
Issue
Foreign location pages par koi local NAP, local phone, local reviews, local backlinks nahi — to ye local relevance ya map-pack place nahi kama sakte. Udhar aapki asli local strength (Jaipur HQ, India/Delhi) Google Business Profile + citations se push nahi ki ja rahi.
Kyun hua
Pages content clones ke roop me bane, local entities ke roop me nahi. Local SEO assets (GBP, citations, reviews) relevant pages se tie nahi kiye gaye.
Solution
  • Leads ka sabse fast rasta: apni Google Business Profile (Jaipur) poori optimize karo, NAP har jagah identical rakho, India citations banao (JustDial, Sulekha, Clutch, GoodFirms, DesignRush), aur actively reviews collect karo. India local hafton me rank ho sakta hai (mahino me nahi), kyunki aap waha genuinely operate karte ho.
  • Jaipur/Delhi/India pages ko truly local banao (local projects, local team, local contact, embedded map).
  • Foreign "…company in X" pages ko market/service pages maano (local office ke bina local pack jeet nahi sakte) — aur sirf wahan jahan dikhane ke liye real clients ho.
MediumGEO / AI search — access achha, citability weak/seo-geo
Issue
AI engines (AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) bhi thin, duplicated pages ko cite nahi karenge. Koi distinctive, quotable facts/stats nahi aur entity signals weak hai.
Kyun hua
Wahi duplication issue + koi unique data points nahi jo AI answer cite karna chahe.
Solution
Crawler access already set hai (robots.txt + llms.txt). Ab unhe cite karne layak kuch do: unique stats, clear process steps, local data, top par concise definition-style passages, aur strong Organization/sameAs entity markup. llms.txt current rakho. Yahan bhi differentiation pehli shart hai.
HighSearch-intent / page-type mismatch (SXO)/seo-sxo
Issue
"mobile app development company in india" ke liye Google ke results me directories (Clutch) aur "Top 15…" listicles chhaye hai plus kuch strong agency pages. "…in new york" ke liye Google deep, dedicated agency location pages ko reward karta hai. Ek thin clone na comparison intent match karta hai na deep-local intent.
Kyun hua
Page type/depth wo nahi hai jo SERP in queries ke liye reward kar raha hai.
Solution
Har kept page ko apni query ke liye genuinely best-in-class banao (depth, proof, specifics, comparison-friendly sections + FAQs). Saath hi wahan list ho jao jahan SERP already point kar raha hai — Clutch, GoodFirms, DesignRush — taaki directory-driven discovery capture ho jo abhi miss ho rahi hai.
MediumImage bloat & page weight (Core Web Vitals risk)/seo-images · /seo-technical · /seo-google
Issue
800–936 KB raw HTML aur 210–232 images per page (tech-stack logos, portfolio & client thumbnails). Heavy DOM. (Alt text zyadatar present hai — wo achhi baat hai.)
Kyun hua
Bade shared template sections har page par inline render ho rahe hai, bahut saari unoptimised images ke saath.
Solution
Below-the-fold images lazy-load karo, WebP/AVIF serve karo, compress & sahi size karo, inline DOM kam karo, aur non-critical assets defer karo. Target: LCP < 2.5s, INP < 200ms, CLS < 0.1. PageSpeed Insights + CrUX se verify karo (apni GSC ke saath /seo-google use karo).
HighPage-level backlinks ≈ 0 (DR 61 domain ke bawajood)/seo-backlinks · /seo-ahrefs
Issue
Naye location pages poori tarah identical footer link block par depend karte hai — inke apne external referring domains lagbhag nahi hai aur URL Rating bahut low hai. (Exact per-URL numbers Ahrefs me confirm karo — is run me Site Explorer plan-locked tha.)
Kyun hua
Pages kabhi promote ya authoritative external/internal sources se link nahi kiye gaye; sitewide footer farm meaningful relevance pass nahi karta.
Solution
  • Sirf 3–5 priority pages ke liye page-level links banao (directories, guest posts, local citations, PR) — 18 par mat phailao.
  • High-value pages se (homepage, blog posts, aur already-ranking main page) priority location pages me contextual internal links daalo, descriptive anchors ke saath.
  • India ke liye: citations + reviews bhi local links ka kaam karte hai.
MediumNew York page par intent/keyword inconsistency/seo
Issue
URL …company-in-new-york-usa hai, title "in USA" kehta hai, aur H1 "New York, USA" kehta hai. Ye ek saath city page bhi banna chahta hai aur country page bhi, to kisi ke liye bhi clear signal nahi. (Main page ka title bhi emoji 📲 se start hota hai aur usme geo nahi — cosmetic hai, blocker nahi.)
Kyun hua
City aur country targeting ek URL me bina clear keyword decision ke merge kar di gayi.
Solution
Har URL ke liye ek intent choose karo. Agar USA market target karna hai to URL/title/H1/content sab "USA" bole. Agar specifically NYC target karna hai to "New York" par commit karo. URL ↔ title ↔ H1 ↔ body ↔ schema align karo.

9. 30 / 60 / 90-day action plan ("hum 6 mahine wait nahi kar sakte" ke liye bana)

Days 0–30 · Bleeding roko + quick wins

  • GSC audit: 18 URLs ka index status, Manual Actions, kya inhe koi impressions milte hai?
  • Keyword map fix karo (§6); NY page ki city/country confusion hatao.
  • Thin clones pause/noindex karo (§7) taaki cluster dilute karna band kare.
  • Schema fix: Organization vs LocalBusiness; self-serving AggregateRating hatao.
  • Google Business Profile (Jaipur) optimize karo + India citations start + happy clients se reviews maango. ← sabse fast lead source.
  • Kept pages par images compress/lazy-load karo.

Days 30–60 · Keepers ko differentiate karo

  • India, Jaipur, Delhi, USA/NY, UK, UAE ko ≥60–70% unique, locally-specific content me rewrite karo: local case studies, named testimonials, local pricing, market specifics, unique FAQs.
  • Ab-localised kept set par hreflang add karo (+ x-default).
  • Homepage / blog / ranking main page se contextual internal links daalo (descriptive anchors).
  • Clutch, GoodFirms, DesignRush par list ho jao.
  • Page genuinely unique ho jaane par dobara indexing request karo.

Days 60–90+ · Authority & expand

  • 3–5 priority pages ke liye page-level link building (PR, guest posts, partnerships).
  • Reviews kamate raho; review velocity banao.
  • GSC me measure karo (impressions → position → clicks) aur jo move kare uspe double down.
  • Tabhi aur country pages dobara introduce karne ka socho — har ek real, unique content + us market ke liye kam se kam ek real client/case study ke saath.
Agar sirf 3 cheezein kar sakte ho to priority order
  1. 11 thin clones pause karo + cannibalisation fix karo (active nuksaan turant rukega).
  2. India local jeeto (GBP + citations + reviews + unique Jaipur/Delhi/India pages) — aapke sabse fast realistic leads.
  3. 2–3 foreign pages ko genuinely unique banao + unpe links banao (longer-term international play).

10. Khud verify karne ki checklist (jo data hum directly nahi nikaal paaye)

Humare paas live page HTML + Ahrefs DR tha, par aapki GSC aur Ahrefs Site Explorer (yahan plan-locked) me confirmation hai. Ye check karo:

  • GSC → Pages (indexing): har 18 URL ka status (expect "Duplicate / chose different canonical" ya "Crawled – not indexed").
  • GSC → URL Inspection → Test live URL 3–4 pages par (Google ka chosen canonical confirm karo).
  • GSC → Manual Actions & Security (doorway/thin-content action rule out karo).
  • GSC → Performance: kya in URLs ko koi impressions milte hai? Zero impressions = filtered, sirf low-rank nahi.
  • Ahrefs → URL Rating + referring domains per page (naye pages ke liye ≈0 expected).
  • Ahrefs/GSC → keyword difficulty + volume aapke target terms ke liye per country (expectations sahi karo).
  • PageSpeed Insights → heavy pages par Core Web Vitals (LCP/INP/CLS).
