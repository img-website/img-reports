4. Baaki issues — problem · kaaran · solution

P0 Service page pe Product + fake-looking AggregateRating

Evidence Head me "@type":"Product" + "aggregateRating": {"ratingValue":"4.7","ratingCount":"142"}.
Kyo matter Service page pe Product type galat + bina visible review system ke self-serving rating Google review-snippet guidelines ke against → spammy structured data risk. (Saari service pages pe common.)
Fix Product + AggregateRating hatao; stars chahiye to real visible reviews + proper markup. Service+Organization rakho.

P1 Bahut saare H2 “slogan” hain — keyword H2 real-estate waste

“We Don't Deliver Apps. We Deliver Personalized Experiences and Innovative Solutions.”
“Deliver Anything. Anytime. Anywhere.”
“We are not just an agency. We are your growth partner.”
“Smart Quick Commerce App Development Solutions With Smart Features” — “Smart…Smart” redundant.
Kyo matter H2 SEO ke liye topic/keyword carry karne chahiye. Inhe slogan banane se generic/salesy lagta hai + topical signal weak hota hai.
Fix Slogan ko sub-text/eyebrow bana do; H2 me keyword-rich topic do (e.g. “Quick Commerce App Features”, “10-Minute Delivery Tech Stack”). “Smart…Smart” fix.

P1 Case studies — Blinkit relevant, baaki ~8 off-topic

Evidence Wahi generic 11 carousel: Blinkit, Astrotalk, Zillow, Uber, Tinder, TikTok, MoneyLion, TaskRabbit, UberEats, Netflix, Practo.
Kyo matter Quick-commerce page pe Blinkit perfect hai (q-commerce/instant delivery), UberEats/Uber tangential — par Tinder/TikTok/Netflix/MoneyLion/Zillow/Astrotalk/Practo unrelated.
Fix Blinkit/Zepto/Instamart/quick-commerce-relevant rakho; baaki link karo.

P1 Sibling cross-link section MISSING

Evidence Food & grocery pages pe “Other On-Demand App Development Services Offered by Us” section hai (siblings ko link karta hai). Is page pe wo section nahi mila.
Kyo matter Internal linking weak → cluster signal + crawl-flow kam.
Fix Yahan bhi “Other On-Demand Services” cross-link section add karo (hub + food/grocery/taxi etc. ko link karo), aur on-demand hub se is page ko link karo.

P1 Naya page hai par refresh nahi hua + on-page E-E-A-T weak

Evidence Schema me datePublished = dateModified = 2025-04-21 (publish ke baad kabhi update nahi). On-page named testimonials/certs/review-link nahi.
Fix Periodic content refresh (real dateModified update); trust band add karo (named testimonials, Clutch/GoodFirms, certs). Naye page ko rank karne me ye dono help karte hain.

P2 Schema/hygiene chhoti baatein

serviceType galat: Service schema ka serviceType list ["AI-Driven Recommendations","Voice-Activated Retail","Smart Cart","Inventory Automation",…] hai — ye features hain, service types nahi. Isme actual services daalo (“Instant Delivery App Development”, “Quick Commerce App Development”, “Dark Store Management”, “Multi-vendor q-commerce”).
Aur: stale .php index (GSC reindex); robots tokens uppercase; image alts vary; possible duplicate Organization node (single @id verify); ~8,500 words + 11-case-study carousel → mobile INP/weight pe dhyan.
