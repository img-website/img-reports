3. Issues — problem · kaaran · solution

P0 Service page pe Product + fake-looking AggregateRating

Evidence Head me "@type":"Product" ke saath "aggregateRating": {"ratingValue":"4.9","ratingCount":"140"}.
Kaaran Product-style template; aap service company ho.
Kyo matter Galat type (yahan koi product bik nahi raha) + bina real on-page review system ke self-serving rating Google ki review-snippet guidelines ke against → “spammy structured data” manual action / stars hatne ka risk. (Yahi on-demand hub aur mobile pages pe bhi mila.)
Fix Product + AggregateRating hatao. Stars chahiye to real visible reviews + proper Review markup; warna Service + Organization (pehle se hai) bina rating ke rakho.

P1 Case studies me 11 me se 8 food se off-topic

Evidence “Our Case Studies Inspiring Innovation” me wahi generic 11 carousel hai: Blinkit, Astrotalk, Zillow, Uber, Tinder, TikTok, MoneyLion, TaskRabbit, UberEats, Netflix, Practo.
Kaaran Saari pages pe same case-study carousel reuse hua hai.
Kyo matter Food delivery page pe Tinder (dating), TikTok (social), Netflix (OTT), MoneyLion (fintech), Zillow (real estate), Astrotalk (astrology), Practo (healthcare) dikhna topical relevance todta hai — sirf UberEats/Blinkit/Uber relevant hain.
Fix Food/delivery-relevant case studies rakho (UberEats, Blinkit, Uber, + agar ho to Zomato/Swiggy/DoorDash type); baaki ko full case-studies page se link karo.

P1 Stats bina source ke + galat scope ka risk

Evidence Page claims $1.40 Trillion Annual Revenue, $1.85 Trillion Expected Market Size By 2029, 7.79% CAGR — koi source nahi.
Accuracy risk Live SERP me food-delivery-app market ~$371B (2024) → $945B (2034) @ 9.8% CAGR dikhta hai. Aapke trillion-$ numbers shayad poore food-service market ko food-delivery-app market se mila rahe hain. Aapne kaha “kuch galat mat batana” — ye exactly wahi risk hai.
Fix Har figure ke saath reputable source + year + clear scope do (food-delivery-app market, total online food market — define karo), ya soften/hata do.

P1 Heading/copy me grammar & generic phrasing

H2: “Build a Food Delivery Apps like Swiggy, Zomato, and Uber Eats” — “a … Apps” galat (ya “a … App”, ya “Food Delivery Apps”).
H2: “The Process We Follow to Develop the Ultimate Food Delivery App Development” — “Develop … Development” redundant.
Hero: “Basically, our solutions can be modified … without any costly rebuild process from scratch.” — filler + wordy.
Detail og:title “…In India” kehta hai par page title global hai — messaging inconsistency.
Fix Headings grammar theek karo, hero ko crisp + specific karo, og:title ko title ke saath align karo (ya India page alag banao).

P1 Google index me abhi bhi purana .php

Evidence site: me food-delivery-app-development.php dikhta hai (301 sahi hai, par index abhi swap nahi hua).
Fix GSC me clean URL pe Request indexing; sitemap se stray legacy .php entry (ek blog item) hatao. 301 rakho — migrate ho jayega.

P2 Chhoti hygiene

Detail Robots tokens uppercase (kaam karta hai, par lowercase convention). Image alt kai jagah heading repeat karte hain — natural/descriptive banao. Possible duplicate Organization node (page @graph + global schema) — single @id verify karo.
