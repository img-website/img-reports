3. Issues — problem · kaaran · solution

P0 Product + AggregateRating (3 pages pe identical = fabricated proof)

Evidence Head me "@type":"Product" + "aggregateRating": {"ratingValue":"4.9","ratingCount":"140"}.
Red flag Yahi 4.9 / 140 food-delivery aur taxi page pe bhi hai — 3 pages identical. Yaani real per-page ratings nahi; clearly hardcoded/fake → Google review-snippet guidelines violation, spammy structured data risk.
Fix Product + AggregateRating hatao; stars chahiye to real visible reviews + proper markup. Service+Organization rakho.

P1 Spelling typo + grammar H2 me (visible mistakes)

H2: “From Initalization to Final Stage: The Development Approach” — typo (“Initialization”).
H2: “Why Select IMG Global Infotech for Your Home Service App Development Company?” — “as Your …” hona chahiye.
Kyo matter Visible spelling/grammar mistakes E-E-A-T & professionalism ko directly hit karte hain (aapne kaha “kuch galat mat batana”).
Fix Typo + grammar theek karo.

P1 Case studies — sirf TaskRabbit relevant, ~10 off-topic (worst ratio)

Evidence Wahi generic 11 carousel: Blinkit, Astrotalk, Zillow, Uber, Tinder, TikTok, MoneyLion, TaskRabbit, UberEats, Netflix, Practo.
Kyo matter Home-service page ke liye sirf TaskRabbit (on-demand home-services marketplace, Urban Company-type) relevant hai — baaki sab (food/dating/OTT/fintech/realestate/astrology/healthcare) unrelated. Ab tak ka sabse kharab relevance ratio.
Fix TaskRabbit (+ Urban Company-type agar ho) rakho; baaki full case-studies page se link karo. (Ya home-service ke real client examples add karo.)

P1 Pehle 3 H2 repetitive/generic “grow your business” framings

“Leverage Your Business with Home Service App Development Solutions”
“Transform the Business Workflow with Custom Home Service App Development Services”
“Enlarge Your Business Reach with Scalable On-Demand Home Service App Development”
Kyo matter Teeno ek hi “business growth” idea + keyword phrase repeat karte hain — generic + thin lagta hai, distinct topical value nahi dete.
Fix Inhe distinct, specific topics banao (e.g. “3-Panel Home Service App (Customer/Provider/Admin)”, “Service Categories You Can Launch”, “Booking, Scheduling & Payments”) — humanized, unique content.

P1 Stale .php index + on-page E-E-A-T

Evidence site: me home-service-app-development.php (301 sahi, index abhi swap nahi). On-page named testimonials/certs/review-link nahi.
Fix GSC me clean URL reindex; trust band add karo (named testimonials, Clutch/GoodFirms, certs).

P2 Chhoti baatein

Detail “Home Service” (title) vs “Home Services” (og/twitter) — singular/plural inconsistency, ek decide karo. “Urban Company Clone” wording thoda low-brand — “Urban Company-like / alternative” better. Image alts vary; robots tokens lowercase; possible duplicate Organization node; lambi page + 11-case-study carousel → mobile INP pe dhyan.
