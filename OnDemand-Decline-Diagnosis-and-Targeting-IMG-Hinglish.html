2. Ranking kyun giri — kaaran (evidence ke saath)

Pehle pattern dekho: page globally Nov 2025 me peak hua (1,298 impressions, pos ~28–34) aur Jan 2026 se gir gaya (541 → 252 → … → 92), India aur US dono me. Aur on-demand + food + grocery — teeno pages ek saath isi time gire. Jab multiple pages, multiple countries me ek hi time pe girein → kaaran algorithmic/sitewide hota hai, page-specific nahi.

1 Sitewide + global drop ~Jan 2026 (sabse bada kaaran — bahut likely Google core update)

Evidence Teeno pages (on-demand/food/grocery) Oct–Nov 2025 peak → Jan 2026 se synchronized decline; on-demand page India + US + saare geos me ek saath gira.
Matlab Dec 2025/Jan 2026 me ya to Google ka core/spam update aaya jisne low-EEAT/generic pages demote kiye, ya koi sitewide change (technical/quality) hua. (Exact update confirm karne ke liye Google update calendar + GSC date-compare check karein.)

2 Page kabhi page-1 pahuncha hi nahi → “discovery boost” ke baad decay

Evidence Best position sirf ~25–28 (page 3) tak gaya, page-1 kabhi nahi. Oct–Dec 2025 me Google ne impressions ka “trial” diya.
Matlab Naye/refreshed content ko Google temporary boost deta hai; agar engagement (clicks) nahi milte to wapas neeche kar deta hai. Yahan bilkul yahi hua.

3 ZERO clicks (0% CTR) — khud ek negative signal + decline ka feedback loop

Evidence Hazaaron impressions, par ~3 clicks total (CTR ~0.1%). Har query pe 0 clicks.
Matlab Google dekhta hai “impressions mil rahe par koi click nahi kar raha” → relevance/quality doubt → position aur neeche. Title/snippet compelling nahi + page-3+ pe hone se CTR ~0 → self-reinforcing giraavat.

4 On-page quality/EEAT weak — core update me sabse pehle yahi pages girti hain

Evidence Generic/hyperbolic content, fake-looking Product + AggregateRating schema, koi named author/testimonials/certs (EEAT) nahi, off-topic case studies (Tinder/Netflix), misplaced “taxi” card, purana radio FAQ.
Matlab Competitors ke comparison me page “thin” hai. Quality-focused update aate hi aise pages demote hote hain. (DR 61 strong hai — to authority problem nahi, page-level quality/EEAT problem hai.)

5 .php ↔ clean URL signal split (contributing)

Evidence GA4: .php abhi bhi clean URL se zyada organic le raha hai; Google index me purana .php. 301 sahi hai par index abhi tak fully migrate nahi hua.
Matlab Ranking signals do URLs me bate hue → consolidation weak → instability. (Ye primary kaaran nahi, par recovery ko slow karta hai.)
Note (honest): exact Google update 100% confirm karne ke liye GSC me “before (Oct–Dec)” vs “after (Feb–May)” date-range compare + known-update dates check karein. Par data ka pattern (multi-page, multi-geo, ek time pe) algorithmic + EEAT/quality ki taraf strongly point karta hai — random ya sirf-technical nahi.

3. Kaha target karein? (India / US / UAE / Global)

Data ke hisaab se clear priority:

🇮🇳 India — PRIMARY (yahi pe focus)

  • Sabse zyada impressions (~2,154) + ~96% actual organic visitors (212 sessions vs US 5) yahi se aate hain.
  • Aap India-based ho + strong India GMB (738 website-clicks/6mo) = local authority/trust ka edge.
  • India-intent queries pe already rank (“on demand app development company india” pos 45, “…company” 443 impr) — push karna realistic.
  • Action: areaServed=India, INR pricing, India market content (Indian startups/SMBs), “…company in India” target, GMB/NAP link, named India clients/testimonials. Yahan ROI sabse zyada + competition winnable.

🇺🇸 United States — SECONDARY (Phase 2, baad me)

  • Latent opportunity: ~1,540 impressions (India ke kareeb!) — demand hai. Recent kuch US queries page-1/2 (pos 6–18) tak aaye, par abhi 0 clicks/sessions (positions zyadatar deep + no US local presence).
  • US market bada + high-value, par competition (Appinventiv etc.) zyada + aapka koi US local edge nahi.
  • Action (baad me): India jeetne ke baad — ya to is page pe US signals/section, ya ek dedicated US-targeted variant. Abhi focus mat baanto.

🇦🇪 UAE / 🌐 Global — ABHI NAHI (is page ke liye)

  • UAE = sirf ~4 impressions is page pe → negligible. (Note: UAE/Gulf quick-commerce demand aapke instant-delivery page pe hai, is on-demand hub pe nahi — usko UAE ke liye optimize karna better.)
  • UK/AU/CA/IT/JP se chhoti scattered impressions aati hain (~40–90 each, deep positions) — dedicated effort worth nahi; general improvements ke saath “free” me aati rahengi.
  • “Globally target” karne se message generic ho jata hai (jaise abhi areaServed: Global) → kisi ek market me strong nahi. Ek market (India) jeeto, phir expand.

One-line answer: India ko primary target karo (sabse zyada impressions + ~saare real visitors + local GMB edge + winnable competition). US ko Phase-2 opportunity rakho (impressions hain par abhi convert nahi ho rahe + competition zyada). UAE/global is page ke liye chhodo (UAE ~4 impressions; UAE ke liye instant-delivery page sahi hai).
