📈 Why this page has never ranked — root causes (WHY + FIX)

1. Top-tier YMYL (stock trading) with zero regulatory / broker / API contentCritical
Why: Trading is the most heavily scrutinised YMYL category Google has. A page selling "stock trading app development" is implicitly asking Google to send buyers who will then build apps that handle real money + real securities. Google's Quality Raters expect: SEBI registration depth, broker-dealer / sub-broker / AP framework, SCRA / Stock Brokers Regulations, KYC / KRA-CKYC, AML/CFT, FATCA / CRS, NSE / BSE membership requirements, custodial accounts, risk disclosure, algo-trading guidelines, F&O margin (SPAN / ELM), Account Aggregator, Smallcase rails — etc. This page covers none of these.
