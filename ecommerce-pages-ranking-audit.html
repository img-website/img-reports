Root causes

Critical 1 · Title & intent mismatch — "app" vs "ecommerce"

URL *-ecommerce-development hai par title/H1 "app development" bol raha hai. Google decide hi nahi kar paata page kis intent ke liye hai — isliye gift page "fnp like app" pe rank karta hai, apne target "gift ecommerce" pe nahi. Shopify title mein "ecommerce" word hi missing hai.

Critical 2 · Keyword cannibalization

ecommerce-development vs ecommerce-website-development-company vs blog — teeno same head term pe ladd rahe. b2c vs d2c bhi ek doosre ko confuse karte (d2c page "b2c ecommerce development" pe pos 95). Blog best-ecommerce-apps ko 16,084 impressions, money pages ko milaakar ~4,000.

High 3 · Purani .php URLs ab bhi index mein

301 redirect laga hai (sahi) par Google ne consolidate nahi kiya — old URL ko aksar zyada impressions / better position mil raha. Equity old/new mein split, koi bhi version page 1 tak nahi pahunchta. Internal links clean hain; ye external backlinks + slow migration ki wajah se hai.

High 4 · Contextual internal link equity = 0

Har page pe ~300 internal links hain par sab header/footer/menu boilerplate. High-traffic blogs (16k + 7k impressions) ki authority money pages tak funnel karne wala koi contextual link nahi. Money pages link-equity ke hisaab se orphan hain.
