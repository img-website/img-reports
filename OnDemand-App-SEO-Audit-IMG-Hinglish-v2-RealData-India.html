5. Page-5 pe kyun atka hai — diagnosis (on-page wajah)

Page rank to karta hai, par page-1 nahi pahunch paa raha. Ye on-page cheezein usse rok rahi hain (pehli report me bhi thi, ab data se justify):

P0 Product + fake AggregateRating (4.7/143) service page pe

Impact Wrong type + self-serving rating bina visible reviews ke → spammy structured data risk; trust/quality signal down. (Saari service pages pe common; food/taxi/home/laundry pe 4.9/140 identical bhi mila = clearly fake.)
Fix Product+AggregateRating hatao; Service+Organization rakho. Stars chahiye to real visible reviews.

P0 Galat “Navigating Taxi Booking App” card “Why choose us” me

Impact Taxi page se copy-paste leftover (sirf is page pe) → topical coherence + trust down.
Fix On-demand-relevant differentiator se replace (e.g. “Multi-vertical on-demand expertise”).

P1 Generic/hyperbolic content + FAQ purana radio (band nahi hota)

Impact “we don’t make apps… crazy results”, “by the on demand delivery app development company” (grammar toota), unsourced stats ($320B/250B+/8.58%) → thin/generic, AI-citation + quality down. FAQ bespoke inline radio markup (naye accordion me nahi aaya).
Fix Intros humanize + specific karo; stats source karo; FAQ ko <details> accordion me lao.

P1 Case studies off-topic + on-page E-E-A-T weak

Impact 11 case studies me Tinder/Netflix/Zillow/MoneyLion (on-demand se unrelated) → topical dilution. Named testimonials/certs/Clutch link nahi. Same-DR (61) pe yahi page-1 vs page-5 ka farak banata hai.
Fix On-demand-relevant case studies rakho; real trust band add karo (testimonials with names, certs, Clutch/GoodFirms).

DATA Stale .php index (ab GA4 + GSC dono se confirmed)

Evidence GA4: .php organic 81 > clean 45. GSC: .php abhi bhi indexed. 301 sahi hai, par Google clean URL ko fully nahi utha raha.
Fix GSC me clean URL Request indexing; internal links clean rakho (hain); decline + .php migration dono GSC se monitor karo.

6. India-targeting — is page ko India ke liye optimize karo 🇮🇳

Data clear hai: impressions India me hain (443 vs 1 US), aur aapka GMB India me strong hai — par page schema “areaServed: Global” kehta hai aur content India-specific nahi. Isko India-first banao:

  • areaServed: Service schema me “India” (ya “India” + “Global”) add karo — real market reflect karo.
  • India content: India market section (Indian startups/SMBs, Tier-1/2 cities), INR pricing (₹) cost range, India compliance (UPI/RBI/GST integrations) — ye India intent + AI answers dono strong karta hai.
  • India trust (E-E-A-T + Local): “Jaipur, India based”, NAP, India clients/case studies, aur GMB profile ka link (738 website-clicks/6mo wala asset) page se jodo.
  • “on demand app development company india” (pos 45, India-intent query) — isko title/H2/content me naturally target karo (abhi page generic hai). Ye India quick-win hai.
  • Internal links: aapke India-ranking blogs + GMB se is page ko link → India relevance + crawl signal.
