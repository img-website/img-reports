3. Issues — problem · kaaran · solution

P0 Product schema me fixed $10,000 InStock Offer + single review + brand typo

Evidence Head ke Product schema me: "offers": { "price":"10000","priceCurrency":"USD","availability":"InStock" }, ek "review" (author “Clutch.co”, rating 4.9), aur brand name "IMGGobalInfotech" (spelling typo — ‘l’ missing).
Kaaran Product-style template; custom service ko “product” ki tarah markup kiya.
Kyo matter (1) Custom service ke liye fixed $10,000 “InStock” Offer misleading hai — Google price/product snippet galat dikha sakta hai. (2) review tab valid hai jab wo review page pe visible ho (sirf markup me hone se guideline violation). (3) Brand typo data-quality issue. (4) Wrong type — yahan koi product bik nahi raha.
Fix Product + Offer hatao. Clutch rating dikhana hai to page pe visible testimonial/review section banao + proper Review/Organization markup; warna sirf Service + Organization (pehle se hai) rakho. Brand name IMGGlobalInfotech theek karo.

P1 Market stats — source + scope ka risk
